Working notes from Trail of Bits' [CTF Field Guide](https://trailofbits.github.io/ctf/), which the course recommends as a resource. This is my own summary in my own words — not a copy of the guide.

## Why the guide is useful

It provides a structured introduction to the major CTF categories and the mindset needed to work through them. Helpful as a checklist when starting a new challenge.

## Sections to revisit

- Introduction and methodology — framing how to approach unknown challenges.
- Vulnerability discovery — mapping observed behavior to bug classes.
- Exploitation — turning a bug into reliable code execution or data disclosure.
- Forensics and reversing — working from artifacts back to behavior.
- Toolkit recommendations — what the guide considers baseline tooling.
